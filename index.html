Ghost artist detection

Who are you really listening to?

Streaming platforms are flooded with AI-generated and ghost-artist tracks — fake accounts with fabricated streaming numbers, collecting royalties from real fans. FanCheck flags suspicious artists in your library so you can make informed decisions about where your money goes.
